Rules & Regulation

STUDENTS
No student may leave the school premises during school hours without a gate pass signed by the Principal. Any default will be viewed very seriously.
Before and after school hours there should be no loitering outside and around the school. School property should not be damaged or destroyed by the students. In case of nay major damage, the student/ students will be liable to pay for the same. The carrying of the following items to school is strictly prohibited, Expensive Watches, Jewellery, large amount of money, iPod, Cellphones (In case of an emergency the student may be allowed to carry a cellphone, however the same is to be handed to the Principal/ School Office). Chewing of gum, Paan Parag or Paan is prohibited.
Pupils whose moral conduct is injurious to the moral tone of the school or incompatible with school’s standard of behavior will be advised to attend counselling classes along with their Parents/ Guardians.
All students should be neatly dressed in full school uniform and should have polished shoe. Hair should be well groomed (No Fancy Hair Styles). Girls having hair longer tan shoulder length, should have their hair plaited. Only black hair-bands to be used. Girl’s should not come to school wearing Mehndi or colored nails.
Students are expected to behave in an orderly manner on their way to and from school. No student is permitted to enter or leave the school premises unless he/ she is wearing his/ her school uniform and ID card.

TEACHERS
The use of mobile phone by teachers during Class/ Teaching hours is not permitted.
Dress Code: Teachers are to be formally dressed. Casuals are not allowed during school working hours.
Teachers to wear their ID-card during school hours.
