Promotion

A student who uses unfair means will not be promoted to the next class irrespective of his/her GP.
A minimum of 80% attendance is required to be promoted to the next class.
A minimum General Proficiency is insufficient for promotion without pass marks in each group of subjects.
Criteria for promotion: Pass marks in each subject for all classes is 40%.
In special circumstances the Monthly Report, in its totality, will also be taken into account in matters of Promotion.
In all matters regarding Promotion the Principal’s decision is final. Requests for reconsideration of a decision will not be entertained.
